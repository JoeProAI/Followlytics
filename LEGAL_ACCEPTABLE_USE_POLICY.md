# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated: November 14, 2024**

## Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines acceptable and prohibited uses of Followlytics. By using our Service, you agree to comply with this policy.

**Violations may result in account suspension, termination, or legal action.**

---

## 1. Acceptable Uses

You MAY use Followlytics for:

### ✅ Legitimate Business Purposes
- Lead generation and sales research
- Competitive intelligence and market analysis
- Talent acquisition and recruitment
- Portfolio tracking and investment research
- Marketing research and audience analysis
- Academic research and data analysis

### ✅ Personal Use
- Understanding your own audience
- Analyzing your follower growth
- Researching accounts in your industry
- Studying social media trends

### ✅ Data Analysis
- Exporting follower data for analysis
- Creating reports and presentations
- Building datasets for research
- Analyzing demographics and patterns

**As long as your use complies with applicable laws and respects others' rights.**

---

## 2. Prohibited Uses

You MAY NOT use Followlytics for:

### ❌ Spam and Harassment
- Sending unsolicited bulk messages (spam)
- Harassing, threatening, or stalking individuals
- Sending malicious or deceptive content
- Creating spam bots or automation tools
- Violating CAN-SPAM, CASL, or anti-spam laws

### ❌ Privacy Violations
- Doxxing or exposing private information
- Stalking or monitoring individuals without consent
- Collecting data for discriminatory purposes
- Violating GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws
- Creating databases of minors

### ❌ Fraudulent Activity
- Identity theft or impersonation
- Phishing or social engineering attacks
- Creating fake accounts or profiles
- Deceptive marketing or scams
- Financial fraud or money laundering

### ❌ Illegal Activities
- Any activity that violates local, state, or federal laws
- Copyright or trademark infringement
- Hacking, unauthorized access, or data breaches
- Distributing malware or viruses
- Terrorism or violent extremism

### ❌ Platform Abuse
- Overloading or disrupting our infrastructure
- Reverse engineering our Service
- Attempting to bypass rate limits or restrictions
- Scraping or copying our Service
- Creating competing services using our data

### ❌ Terms of Service Violations
- Violating X (Twitter) Terms of Service
- Violating other social media platform terms
- Using data in ways prohibited by platforms
- Evading platform restrictions or bans

### ❌ Unethical Uses
- Discrimination based on race, religion, gender, etc.
- Political suppression or voter manipulation
- Exploiting vulnerable populations
- Revenge or malicious targeting
- Creating misinformation campaigns

---

## 3. Specific Prohibitions

### You May NOT:

**Resell or Redistribute:**
- Resell exported data as a competing service
- Redistribute data to third parties for profit
- Create derivative services using our infrastructure

**Abuse Rate Limits:**
- Make excessive requests to overwhelm systems
- Use automated scripts to bypass limits
- Share accounts to multiply access

**Misrepresent Data:**
- Falsely claim data is from official sources
- Alter data and present it as authentic
- Use data to defame or harm others

**Violate Consent:**
- Contact individuals who have not consented (where required)
- Ignore opt-out requests
- Violate "Do Not Contact" lists

---

## 4. Data Usage Requirements

### If You Use Exported Data, You MUST:

**Comply with Laws:**
- GDPR (if contacting EU residents)
- CCPA (if contacting California residents)
- CAN-SPAM Act (for commercial emails)
- CASL (for Canadian contacts)
- TCPA (for phone contacts in US)

**Respect Privacy:**
- Use data only for stated purposes
- Implement appropriate security measures
- Honor opt-out and deletion requests
- Provide clear privacy notices

**Be Transparent:**
- Disclose how you obtained data (if asked)
- Don't misrepresent your relationship with individuals
- Provide unsubscribe options (for emails)

- Legal action if warranted

**Severe Violations:**

- Report to law enforcement
- Legal action for damages
- Cooperation with authorities

We reserve the right to:

## 9. Best Practices

### We Recommend:

**For Sales/Marketing:**
- Only contact people relevant to your offering
- Provide clear opt-out options
- Honor opt-outs immediately
- Personalize messages (avoid looking like spam)
- Follow industry best practices

**For Recruitment:**
- Only contact qualified candidates
- Be transparent about job opportunities
- Respect "not interested" responses
- Comply with employment laws

**For Research:**
- Anonymize data when possible
- Obtain IRB approval if required
- Follow academic research ethics
- Cite data sources appropriately
