# Refund Policy

**Last Updated: November 14, 2024**

## Our Commitment

At Followlytics, we want you to be satisfied with our Service. This Refund Policy explains when and how refunds are issued.

## 1. Refund Eligibility

### You Are Eligible for a Full Refund If:

✅ **Export Failed Completely**
- The export did not complete
- No data was delivered
- Technical error prevented export

✅ **Service Unavailable**
- Service was down during your paid export
- Unable to process your request due to our technical issues

✅ **Incorrect Export**
- Exported wrong account
- Data corruption or format issues
- File unreadable or unusable

✅ **Duplicate Charge**
- Accidentally charged twice for same export
- Unauthorized or fraudulent charge

### You Are NOT Eligible for a Refund If:

❌ **Export Completed Successfully**
- Data was delivered as described
- File downloaded successfully
- You changed your mind after receiving data

❌ **Account Limitations**
- Target account is private/protected
- Target account has blocked scraping
- Platform rate limits affected export (we still delivered partial data)

❌ **User Error**
- Entered wrong username
- Didn't download file within 30 days
- Lost or deleted your export

❌ **Dissatisfaction with Public Data**
- Data is less valuable than expected
- Follower quality is poor (we export what exists)
- Account has fewer followers than anticipated

❌ **Terms of Service Violation**
- You violated our Terms of Service
- You used data illegally
- You abused the Service

## 2. Free Tier Exports

Exports for accounts under 500 followers are FREE. No refunds apply since no payment was made.

## 3. How to Request a Refund

### Steps:
1. **Contact us within 7 days** of purchase
2. **Email:** [your-email@followlytics.com]
3. **Include:**
   - Your order/transaction ID
   - Username you exported
   - Reason for refund request
   - Description of the issue

### Response Time:
We will respond within **48 hours** (business days).

### Processing Time:
Approved refunds are processed within **5-10 business days**.

## 4. Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**:
- Credit/debit card refunds via Stripe
- Refund appears on your statement within 5-10 business days
- PayPal refunds (if applicable) within 3-5 business days

**We do NOT issue refunds via:**
- Cash
- Check
- Different payment method than original
- Gift cards or store credit

## 5. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds:

### 50% Refund:
- Export partially completed (e.g., 50% of followers extracted)
- Minor data issues that don't prevent use
- Significant delays but export eventually delivered

### Decision:
Partial refund amounts are at our sole discretion based on the circumstances.

## 6. Disputes and Chargebacks

### Before Filing a Chargeback:
Please contact us first. Most issues can be resolved quickly.

### If You File a Chargeback:
- Your account may be suspended
- We will provide evidence to the payment processor
- Fraudulent chargebacks may result in legal action
- You may be liable for chargeback fees

**We prefer to resolve issues directly with you.**

## 7. Special Circumstances

### Platform Outages:
If X (Twitter) or other platforms are down and prevent export, we will:
- Retry automatically when service resumes
- Offer refund if retry fails
- Extend download period

### API Changes:
If platforms change their APIs and break our Service:
- We will fix issues as quickly as possible
- Refunds issued if we cannot deliver
- No refunds for temporary disruptions (<24 hours)

### Force Majeure:
We are not liable for refunds due to events beyond our control (natural disasters, wars, government actions, etc.).

## 8. Satisfaction Guarantee

### Our Promise:
If the Service fails to deliver the export due to our technical issues, you get your money back. No questions asked.

### What This Means:
- We stand behind our Service
- If we mess up, we make it right
- Fair and transparent refund process

## 9. No Recurring Charges

Followlytics uses **pay-per-export** pricing:
- No subscriptions
- No recurring charges
- No cancellation fees

You only pay when you request an export.

## 10. Refund Exceptions

Refunds may be denied for:
- Abuse of refund policy (requesting refunds repeatedly)
- Fraudulent activity
- Terms of Service violations
- Requests made >30 days after purchase
